# Journal Cartridge — Entry Format Guide

## Purpose

This cartridge holds personal journal entries, logs, and reflections indexed for local
retrieval. Entries are timestamped notes that the Heart can reference in reflective
conversation.

---

## Entry Format

Each journal entry should follow this structure:

```
## YYYY-MM-DD — Title (optional)

Body text of the entry. Can be any length. Multiple paragraphs are fine.

Tags: observation, reflection, project, technical, personal
```

---

## What Belongs Here

- Daily observations and notes
- Project logs and progress tracking
- Reflections on creative or technical work
- Questions to return to
- Links between ideas discovered in practice

---

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Unverified external material (use Threshold)
- Active working drafts (use Workshop)
- Reference documents (use appropriate knowledge cartridge)

---

## Retrieval Notes

Journal entries are available to the Heart for reflective conversation. When you ask
about patterns in your own work or thinking, the Heart can draw on journal entries
as local evidence.

The Heart does not fabricate journal entries. It retrieves what has actually been written.
